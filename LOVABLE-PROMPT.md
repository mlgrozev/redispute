Build a modern, conversion-optimized landing page for **ReDispute** - a Shopify payment hold prevention and chargeback management platform targeting Shopify merchants with $100K-$2M monthly revenue.

## Design Requirements

### Visual Style
- **Modern SaaS aesthetic**: Clean, professional, trustworthy
- **Color scheme**:
  - Primary: Deep blue (#1E40AF) - trust and security
  - Secondary: Green (#10B981) - success and safety
  - Accent: Orange (#F97316) - urgency and alerts
  - Background: White with subtle gray sections (#F9FAFB)
- **Typography**:
  - Headlines: Bold, sans-serif (Inter or similar)
  - Body: Clean, readable (16px minimum)
  - Generous line spacing for readability
- **Visual hierarchy**: Clear distinction between headlines, subheadlines, and body text
- **Whitespace**: Generous padding and margins - don't cram content

### Layout
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design (60%+ traffic from mobile)
- **Above the fold**: Hero section must be impactful on all screen sizes
- **Sticky CTA**: Floating "Start Free Trial" button on mobile scroll
- **Navigation**: Simple header with logo, Features, Pricing, Demo CTA
- **Footer**: Comprehensive with links, social, legal
