## üîç KEY PAIN POINTS (Disruption Opportunities)

### 1. **Small Business Abandonment**
- Current solutions optimize for $1M+ annual revenue brands
- Small businesses can't justify 20% success fees on low-margin products
- Manual processes eat 20-60 minutes per chargeback

### 2. **Friendly Fraud Epidemic** (70%+ of chargebacks)
- 84% of customers find chargebacks easier than requesting refunds
- 72% think chargebacks = refunds
- **No current solution addresses this at the customer level**

### 3. **Template Theater (Not Real AI)**
- Disputifier uses pre-built templates, not adaptive AI
- Rigid rule-based workflows can't evolve with fraud tactics
- High false positive rates damage legitimate transactions

### 4. **Evidence Collection Burden**
- Merchants spend 20-60 minutes gathering evidence per dispute
- Manual process delays responses
- Missing evidence = automatic loss

### 5. **Pricing Opacity & Risk**
- Many providers hide pricing behind sales calls
- Unexpected billing (Disputifier's $799 surprise charge issue)
- Performance fees reach 25% with no caps

### 6. **Security Vulnerabilities**
- Disputifier breach (Jan 2026) exposed API tokens
- Unauthorized refunds issued
- Loss of merchant trust

- Predict chargeback risk at checkout (not just post-purchase)

**Features:**
- Real-time risk scoring with explainable AI
- Continuous learning from outcomes
- Personalized fraud thresholds per merchant
- A/B testing fraud rules automatically
